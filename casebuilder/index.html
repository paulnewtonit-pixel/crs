Build your case step by step

What happens next?

A controlled five-stage process

  1. 1Select or create a case
  2. 2Complete the case foundation
  3. 3Tell CourtReady what happened
  4. 4Review identified facts, issues and evidence gaps
  5. 5Publish only approved records to McKenzieCMS
