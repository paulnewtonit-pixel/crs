Hearings

Remote and video hearings

Practical steps for phone, video and hybrid hearings, including documents, technology and privacy.

Practical guide CourtReadySupport practical guide

Technical checks

  • Test your device, camera, microphone, charger, internet connection and the platform link before the hearing day.
  • Keep the court telephone number available in case the link fails.
  • Join early and use your full name so the court can identify you.

Documents during a remote hearing

  • Have the bundle open and easy to search.
  • Keep a paper note of key page numbers in case switching screens becomes awkward.
  • Avoid sending documents during the hearing unless the court asks for them or the directions allow it.

Privacy and conduct

  • Use a quiet private room where others cannot overhear confidential information.
  • Do not record the hearing unless the court has given permission.
  • Stay muted when not speaking and wait for the judge to invite you in.

Important note

Remote hearing rules and platform arrangements can vary. Follow the joining instructions from the court.
