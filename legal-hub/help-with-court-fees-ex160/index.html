Fees

Help with court fees and EX160

What to gather before applying for help with court fees and how to keep the application organised.

Practical guide CourtReadySupport practical guide

What EX160 is for

  • EX160 is used to apply for help with court or tribunal fees where you may qualify financially.
  • You may need details of income, benefits, savings, dependants and the court fee.
  • The application can affect whether a fee is paid, reduced or remitted.

Before applying

  • Check the current eligibility rules.
  • Gather benefit letters, payslips, bank information or other proof requested by the form.
  • Make a note of the court fee and the form or application it relates to.

Keep records

  • Save the reference number or confirmation.
  • Keep copies of evidence submitted.
  • Track whether the court has accepted the application before assuming the fee issue is resolved.

Important note

Eligibility and evidence requirements can change. Check the current official guidance before applying.
