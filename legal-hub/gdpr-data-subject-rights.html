Individuals have rights under UK GDPR in relation to their personal data.

Your rights

You may request to access your personal data, request correction, request erasure where lawful, restrict processing, object to processing, and request data portability where applicable.

How to make a request

Requests should be submitted using the contact details on this website. We may need to verify identity before acting on a request.

Timeframes

We aim to respond within one month, subject to any lawful extension where a request is complex or numerous.

Limitations

Some rights may be limited where retention is required by law, court process, security, dispute handling, audit records or legitimate business purposes.
